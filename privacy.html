🔐 Privacy & Anonymity

How we keep your identity safe while you share your truth 💖

🏠 Home 📖 Stories 🛡️ Report

💬 Story Sharing

When you share a story, your name, email, and any other identifying data are not collected. All stories are stored anonymously using Google Firestore.

📄 Harassment Reports

The report form is powered by Google Forms, and it's set to NOT collect email addresses or logins. No response receipts. No IP logs. Just your voice.

🧠 Who Can See This?

Only the SheCode team will be able to view the backend data. Your identity stays between you and your truth.

🛑 You Control the Narrative

You are never required to share anything you’re not ready to. This is YOUR space, YOUR story, YOUR boundaries.
